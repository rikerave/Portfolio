Alo: Client Presntation

This is the third and final step to my last two previous projects. Again it was for my MKT 419 class and is based around the brand Alo. I had to take what I came up with in the creative breif and put it into a presenation and simulate what it would be like to pitch it Alo themselves. I created a lengthy powerpoint to present to my professor. It had about 16 slides and consisted of the holiday Launch idea and decriptions and visuals of all the products and colors I created. I made sure to make the presenation professional yet fun so I decided to choose a festive background for my slides to go along with the Holiday theme. I am very proud of how this project turned out and looking at all three parts, it shows how much effort I put into my work and how dedicated I am to making it look serious and professional.

Challenges and Learnings

The most challenging part of this assingment for me was coming up with facts to put on the slides about the Alo and their sales. Not only did I have to present my idea and show visuals of what I want the launch to look like, I also needed to present that sales aspect of it. Doing research of Alo's past ijncrease of sales was a bit difficult just beacsue there wasn't a ton of information on it. Ovrall, I liked how it turned out and I felt like I had enough information to make it sound accurate and professional.

Alo:Social Audit

This project was for my MKT 419. This project had me pick a brand (Alo) and study their social media and marketing tactics and put it into a neat and professional presentation. Work like this is typically called a "Soaicl Media Audit." To create a professional yet chic loook for my information, I used Adobe Express.
